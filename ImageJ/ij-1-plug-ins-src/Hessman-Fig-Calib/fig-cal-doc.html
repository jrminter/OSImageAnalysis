Figure_Calibration

Author: Frederic V. Hessman (hessman at Astro.physik.Uni-Goettingen.de)
Inst. f. Astrophysik, Georg-August-Universität Göttingen
Date:2009-08-12
Source: Figure_Calibration.java
Installation: Download Figure_Calibration.class to the plugins folder and restart ImageJ.
Description: If you draw a rectangle defining a region in a graph stored as an image, Figure_Calibration will ask you for the coordinates of the corners and apply them to the figure so that you can read off the numbers (e.g. using a point selection and Measure).
Example: Here I am calibrating the scale on a figure taken from the astronomical literature (S.S. McGaugh in IAU Symposium 244, 2007). I previously place a rectangular ROI on the graph borders and have filled in the X- and Y-coordinates of the box's lower left and upper right coordinates (here as the logarithms since the plot is actually logarithmic).
